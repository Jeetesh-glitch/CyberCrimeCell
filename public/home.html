About This Platform

Why it exists: In today's digital age, misinformation and fake news can cause significant harm to society, leading to panic, violence, and erosion of trust. This platform serves as a crucial defense mechanism to prevent such harm.

Management: This initiative is managed by the Police Cyber Crime Unit in collaboration with the Government IT Cell, ensuring professional oversight and rapid response capabilities.

Our Mission

"To detect, prevent, and counter harmful social media impact for a safer society through collaborative efforts between law enforcement and citizens."
