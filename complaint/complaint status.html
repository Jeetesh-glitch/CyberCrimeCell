Frequently Asked Questions

Didn't get your Complaint ID?

Your complaint ID is sent to your registered email/phone immediately after filing. Check your email spam folder or SMS. If still not found, contact our helpline at 1930 with your registration details.

How long does resolution take?

Resolution time varies based on complaint complexity:

  • Simple cases: 7-15 days
  • Financial fraud: 15-30 days
  • Complex cybercrime: 30-60 days

You'll receive regular updates throughout the process.

Who handles my complaint?

Complaints are assigned to specialized units based on the type of cybercrime:

  • Financial Fraud: Economic Offences Wing
  • Identity Theft: Cyber Investigation Unit
  • Online Harassment: Women & Child Safety Division
  • Data Breach: Technical Investigation Team

Can I update my complaint after filing?

Yes, you can submit additional information using the "Submit Additional Info" button after checking your status. You can also call our helpline for urgent updates.
